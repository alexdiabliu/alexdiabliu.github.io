Summary


Mentis is an all-inclusive mental health chatbot designed to allow people with symptoms of psychological distress an opportunity to freely express themselves. Mentis is able to offer a variety of solutions to any specific problem regarding mental health. One of said features include recommended activities and locations, allowing the user to freely express themselves, and offering elaborate exercises to relieve stress. Mentis also allows for the user to book an appointment at a nearby clinic with professional therapists - experts who are trained in dealing with mental health. It is this all-in-one feature as well as its quality of solutions that separates Mentis from its competitors.




Mental Health


With over 970 million people worldwide as of 2021 currently suffering from an illness related to mental health, people are feeling increasingly anxious and depressed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Although many mental health services are being offered, there are still many barriers preventing the general public from getting the help they need.
