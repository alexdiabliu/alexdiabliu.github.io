Summary


Vestomy is the heavy-duty ostomy belt built for durability. It allows for its users to undergo daily physical activity without having the need to worry about the longevity or comfortability of their ostomy belt.

The secure connection provided by our press-and-twist mechanism as well as the security from the vest allow for greater security while the storage across the belt allows for a longer lifespan.


Case Study


The case study assigned to our team described a man who has encountered numerous troubles with his ostomy pouch disconnecting during his physically taxing job or during his daily physical activity. Our team was tasked to design a colostomy bag that provides a durable connection between the drainable pouch and the skin barrier. In addition, the device should maintain comfortability for long periods of physical activity. We wanted the patient/user to be as comfortable as possible in their environment and not to feel limited by their ostomy.
